Bell Classics specialise in selling, restoring and servicing all Ferrari models, and also the restoration of Aston Martin DB4/5/6s.

Formed in 1989, our passion and enthusiasm have helped us grow into one of the country’s leading independent Ferrari specialists; the skills and experience of our factory-trained technicians allowing us to carry out restoration work to Concours d’Elegance standard. Those same skills also allow us to offer comprehensive servicing and maintenance, essential for owners who want to fully appreciate and enjoy their cars.

Our Sales department offer a selection of Ferraris and Aston Martins, including restoration projects that we can finish to your own specification; we also have specialised engineering and R&D facilities, and sell a small selection of specialised components.

But, like you, first and foremost, we’re enthusiasts! So why not drop in, have a coffee, meet us and see for yourself?
